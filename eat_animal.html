Tangled: Choose Your Own Adventure!

Since you are so hungry, you decide to kill and eat the strange animal. You take an axe out of your purse and chop the animal's head off in a single blow. Then, you chop the animal up, start a fire and roast the meat over it. It smells amazing, even though the meat is a strange green color. You take a bite of the meat and immediately start choking. You can't breathe! You start seizing up and die in less than a minute. No one will ever find your body.
